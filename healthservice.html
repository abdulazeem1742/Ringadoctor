Introduction

Ring a Doctor is Pakistan’s first Healthcare-as-a-Service (HaaS) Provider. With its association with Integrated Health Services (IHS), Islamabad – Ring a Doctor’s scope of services is expansive. With over a 100, PMDC-verified doctors on the panel, and 22 years of on-ground experience, we were able to positively touch the lives of over 20 million Pakistanis since 1996.

Following are the services offered via the Ring a Doctor platform:

    24/7 Online Video Consultations – Ring a Doctor

  • With over a 100 doctors, Ring a Doctor is a platform that connects patients with doctors over live video, round the clock.
  • Access to specialists, GP-level doctors is easy from Ring a Doctor.
  • You also receive a prescription (if needed) at the end of a consultation which is printable
  • You can book appointments with doctors, either online or physically
  • A doctor can even be booked for a home visit should the need arise

    24/7 Home Medical Care

  • Via Ring a Doctor, you can book a doctor’s visit to your home. Apart from a doctor we also provide long-term home-based patient care via patient or medical attendants.
  • Home Medical Care uses a dedicated UAN and Cell Numbers which means having a doctor, medic, nurse or an ambulance to your place of concern is easier.

    G-8 Markaz Clinic

  • If you feel an online doctor consultation was not enough, you can always see our doctors in our G-8 Markaz Clinic in Islamabad. The clinic operates 24/7.
  • From the clinic, if the need arises, a patient may be transported to a hospital using the fleet of ambulances at our disposal.

    F-10 Mother & Child Hospital

  • A Mother & Child Hospital in F-10 is at your disposal should your child fall ill.
  • The best doctors in Islamabad are serving in the hospital providing quality and reliable care for your young ones.

    G-8 Institute of Medical Rehabilitation

  • We offer a host of psychologists, physiotherapists, speech therapists and several more rehabilitation services at IMR in G-8 Markaz.

    24/7 Medicine Delivery

  • With Ring a Doctor, you can be certain that the prescribed medicines will be delivered to you at your doorstep should you require.
  • You can put a request for delivery as soon as you receive the prescription from the doctor.

    Electronic Medical Records

  • Save your medical history online, complete with detailed reports.
  • Your prior, as well as online history with Ring a Doctor can be saved which is retrievable for an indefinite amount of time and securely saved over the cloud.

    Hospital Facilitation

  • If you were referred to a hospital via Ring a Doctor, you can call us to facilitate you with the formalities at the hospital
  • We will make your stay in the hospital as comfortable as we can

    24/7 Labs

  • In a situation where you were prescribed lab tests, we can not only have the samples collected from your home and have the results delivered when ready, but also offer you significant discounts at major labs including IDC and Excel.
